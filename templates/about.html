About

Dragon-Drop is a bookmarking site. Our ranking filter is applied to your search results to increase suitability of responses.
    We have:
  • Prioritisation of bookmarks within folders.
  • Instances of the site within bookmarks.
  • Overall site preferences.

This Web Application has been developed as a part of MScIT Internet Technology project.
